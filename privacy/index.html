Privacy Policy • Last updated: May 2026

VerifyGF is designed to help users identify possible gluten-related concerns in packaged food products. Your privacy is important, and the app is designed to minimize data collection whenever possible.

Information We Collect

VerifyGF does not require an account and does not collect personal profile information such as your name, email address, precise location, or health profile.

The app does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Camera Access

VerifyGF uses your device camera solely for barcode scanning and reading ingredient labels. Barcode video frames, captured ingredient photos, and recognized label text are processed on-device by the app. Camera images, captured ingredient photos, and video are not stored or uploaded by the app.

Barcode and Product Lookup Requests

When you scan a barcode, the barcode number may be sent to external food databases in order to retrieve product information. Like any internet communication, these requests inherently transmit standard technical metadata, such as your device's IP address, to the receiving server for the sole purpose of fulfilling the network request.

Open Food Facts

Barcode lookup requests may be sent directly to Open Food Facts to retrieve publicly available product information, including ingredients, allergens, labels, and product metadata.

USDA FoodData Central

If a product is not available through Open Food Facts, VerifyGF may send the barcode number through a Cloudflare Worker service that securely forwards the request to USDA FoodData Central. That service is used only to forward barcode lookup requests to USDA FoodData Central.

VerifyGF does not sell these requests, use them for advertising, or use them to track users across apps or websites.Hosting infrastructure may process temporary request metadata for security, debugging, abuse prevention, and service reliability.

These requests are used only to retrieve product information associated with the scanned barcode.

Local Device Storage

VerifyGF stores recent scans and product cache data locally on your device to improve app performance and usability.

This information is not used to identify you and is not shared or sold by the app.

Website

VerifyGF uses Cloudflare Pages to host its informational website and universal-link pages used for app sharing and deep linking. As part of normal web infrastructure operations, Cloudflare may process limited technical request information such as IP addresses, browser metadata, caching, and security logs. VerifyGF does not use advertising trackers or third-party marketing analytics on these pages.

Medical Disclaimer

VerifyGF provides informational gluten-risk screening only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Product ingredients, manufacturing processes, and labeling may change over time. Always verify packaging labels and manufacturer information, especially for celiac disease or severe allergies.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or VerifyGF, you can contact the developer at verifygf@icloud.com.
