VERIFYGF
A completely free barcode and ingredient label scanner for gluten-free checking.

VerifyGF was built to be simple, useful, and straightforward. No accounts. No subscriptions. No tracking. No ads.

Why I Made It

I was recently diagnosed with celiac disease and wanted a simple app that could help me scan the food already in my pantry to figure out what was safe and what ingredients I should start learning to watch out for.

Every app I tried was overloaded with ads or required monthly or even weekly subscriptions. I finally got fed up and decided to make my own.

Scan a Barcode or Ingredient Label

Scan a barcode to look up product information using Open Food Facts. If information is missing, VerifyGF can fall back to USDA FoodData Central when available.

If a product is not in the databases, you can scan the ingredient label directly with the built-in OCR scanner.

Built for U.S. Labels

VerifyGF checks ingredients, allergen statements, gluten-free labels, certifications, and common warning signs using United States labeling standards and guidance.

Ways to Double-Check

Product databases can be incomplete or outdated. VerifyGF includes manual verification tools so you can search the product, manufacturer, or specific ingredients when something needs a closer look.

Private by Design

VerifyGF does not require an account and does not collect personal profiles, health information, or precise location data.

Recent scans and cached products stay stored locally on your device.

Privacy Policy

Important Disclaimer

VerifyGF is an informational screening tool only and does not guarantee a product is gluten-free or safe for individuals with celiac disease or allergies.

Ingredients and manufacturing processes can change over time. Always verify packaging labels and manufacturer information before consuming a product.
